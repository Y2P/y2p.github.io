Yusuf Yigit PILAVCI

I am was a PhD student in the GAIA team of GIPSA Lab under the supervision of Nicolas Tremblay, Simon Barthelmé and Pierre-Olivier Amblard. In my thesis, I am working on Monte Carlo algorithms for linear algebra based on a particular stochastic process on graphs, called random spanning forests.

Breaking News! I defended my thesis on 15 November 2022. See here for more details.

Interests
